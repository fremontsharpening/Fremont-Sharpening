Create a professional website for "Fremont Sharpening," a mobile knife and tool sharpening service targeting wealthy home cooks in the local area. The website should convey a professional, local craftsman aesthetic that builds trust and expertise.
Business Overview:
Fremont Sharpening is a mobile sharpening service that comes directly to customers' homes to sharpen their knives and tools. The service is convenient, professional, and caters to home cooks who value quality kitchen tools.
Website Goals:

Generate bookings through a prominent link to the booking site
Provide clear information about services offered
Establish credibility and professionalism
Make it easy for customers to understand pricing and process

Required Pages:

Home/Info Page - Overview of the service, benefits of mobile sharpening, professional credentials, service area
Price Sheet - Clear pricing for different items (see pricing details below)
FAQ - Common questions about the sharpening process, scheduling, payment, turnaround time
Booking Integration - Highly visible and easy-to-find link/button to https://fremontsharpening.youcanbook.me/ throughout the site

Pricing Structure:

Kitchen Knives: $9
Cleavers/Large Knives (10+ in): $12
Scissors: $9
Serrated Knives: $9
Machetes: $16
Axes/Hatchets: $16
Pruning Shears: $12
Kitchen Tools (Pizza Cutters, Food Processor Blades, Peelers, etc.): $12
Large Loppers/Lawnmower Blades: $16 (Note: Free removal on mower blades!)

Design Style:

Professional and trustworthy aesthetic
Local craftsman/artisan feel
Clean, uncluttered layout
Imagery suggesting precision, quality, and care
Warm but professional color palette (consider earth tones, metallics, or classic combinations)

Key Features:

Mobile-responsive design (must work perfectly on phones and tablets)
Prominent "Book Now" or "Schedule Service" call-to-action buttons on every page linking to the booking site
Before/after imagery opportunities
Trust signals (experience, certifications, service area, testimonials if available)
Clear contact information
Easy-to-read pricing table/grid on the pricing page

Tone:
Professional yet approachable. Emphasize convenience, expertise, and quality results. Appeal to discerning home cooks who invest in quality kitchen tools and want them maintained properly.
